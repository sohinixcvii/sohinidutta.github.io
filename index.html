About Me

I completed my Master's degree in Astronomy from the Department of Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology, Indore. My present research interests lie in the field of Cosmology and the very early universe, particularly in the areas of Inflation, Galaxy Evolution, Cosmic Dawn, the Epoch of Reionization etc. I worked as a Data Scientist at American Express and now, I am looking forward to joining the Jodrell Bank Centre of Astrophysics at the University of Manchester as a PhD student. If you would like to chat with me, find my email in the navigation bar!

Research Interests
